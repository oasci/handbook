# Personas

Personas are a generalized way of talking about the ideal target we are aiming to communicate with and design for.
They help us not only define our messaging and marketing delivery, but also our product.
Keeping personas in mind allows us to use the correct language and make the best decisions to address their specific problems and pain points.
